How many siblings does Alix Earle have?

ALIX Earle quickly rose to fame for her relatable TikTok content and now boasts millions of followers across multiple online platforms.

Now, fans want to know more about the social media sensation's family.

How many siblings does Alix Earle have?

Alix Earle has a total of four siblings, including one sister, two half-sisters, and one half-brother.

Prior to their divorce, Alix Earle's parents — Thomas and Alisa Earle — welcomed her and her younger sister, Ashtin Earle.

After their split, Thomas married Alix's stepmom, Ashley Dupré, in 2013, and the couple share three children.

Per Hollywood Life, their names are Izabel Earle, Penelope Earle, and Thomas James Earle II.

As People reported, in 2008, news broke that Alix's dad was having an affair with Ashley while he was married to Alisa.

Ashley had previously gained media attention for a sex scandal involving former New York Governor Eliot Spitzer.

While speaking on a September 20, 2023, episode of the Call Her Daddy podcast, Alix opened up about how Thomas and Ashely's affair impacted her now-close family.

When the news broke of the affair, she arrived home from elementary school to find paparazzi at her family's property.

Alisa promptly took her and Ashtin to their cousin's house for a few days.

The following year, Alisa and Thomas told Alix that they were splitting up.

The influencer revealed that while in fourth grade, she found out that her family was in the media, which made her feel like she was living a "lie."

She said on the podcast: "The past two years of my life had been a lie.

"I had been reading articles about what had happened, about the times my dad has taken me to go get ice cream and it’s really because there’s paparazzi outside my house and he’s hiding us from them.

"I felt like my world crashed down in that moment."

The TikTok star noted that Alisa was "super mature" about the situation and also opened up about being bullied at school.

However, despite the challenges, Alix has shared that her family is now close.

She said on the podcast: "Now that my family is so well blended and they get along so well I get to do all these events in my life where it’s my mom, my dad, my little siblings, and my stepmom.

"It’s a new feeling because it’s a different type of family to me but having my mom around with my dad, it’s just that feeling is the best feeling in the entire world."

Alix also opened up about her "modern family" in an August 2023 interview with Elle, saying: "We definitely have a very modern family.

"It’s normal for me to have my dad, my mom, and my stepmom all going on the same trips."

Who is Alisa Earle?

Alisa is raising her two daughters, Alix and Ashtin.

According to her LinkedIn page, she is a stay-at-home mom based in Farmingdale, New Jersey.

She is involved with a variety of philanthropies and charities in the New Jersey area, such as the C.H.A.S.E for Life Foundation.

Alisa's profile states. "Founded in 2005, the C.H.A.S.E for Life Foundation teaches critical lifesaving skills such as infant/child CPR and choking maneuvers to as many people as possible with the ultimate goal of reducing accidental deaths from drowning and choking to zero."

She often spends time supporting the Meridian Health Hospitals of New Jersey, "one of the state’s most important health organizations."

The mom-of-two also enjoys working out, horseback riding, and dancing.

Also per her LinkedIn, Alisa attended Lynn University from 1992 to 1995, where she earned a bachelor's degree in fashion marketing.
