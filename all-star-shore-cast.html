What is the All Star Shore grand prize?

Whoever defeats the other contestants and is crowned the winner gets to take home All Star Shore's $150,000 prize.

To win, the reality stars will have to outsmart each other in "epic party-style challenges," to escape elimination.

How can I watch All Star Shore?

All Star Shore season 2 premieres at 9pm EST on September 21, 2023, on MTV.

For fans who would like to catch on what they missed on season 1, the series is available to stream on Paramount+ and Amazon Prime Video.
