Amazon is selling a $6,000 barn-style tiny home with a second level and 50 year warranty

GOOD news for people looking for more room at home.

Amazon is selling a tiny home kit for $6,025 which will provide extra living space.

The Best Barns Millcreek model is a wood shed kit.

The kit comes with pre-cut and pre-drilled pieces.

The siding for the outside comes with a 50 year warranty.

Best Barns said all of their products are made in the USA.

Read more tiny home stories

If you're interested in this tiny home shed kit, the dimensions run 240 x 144 x 144 inches.

For Amazon Prime members, you can get it delivered for free within 13 to 14 days.

Availability and delivery times may vary depending on where you live.

WHAT THEY'RE SAYING

The Millcreek model has received either five stars or three stars.
