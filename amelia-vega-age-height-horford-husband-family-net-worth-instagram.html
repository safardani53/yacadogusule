What Does Amelia Vega Do?- Miss Universe Life

Modeling and glamor have always been part of Amelia Vega’s life. It is fair to say that her career in the entertainment world officially started after winning the Miss Dominican Republic title back in 2003.

As Miss Universe, Vega worked extensively with internationally recognized HIV/AIDS organizations such as the Global Health Council, Cable Positive, amfAR, and many others.

With her help, the organizations generated millions in funding and increased awareness among the people, especially in her own country.

Aside from that, Vega also represented the Miss Universe Organization. Along with the main event, the 17 years also grabbed the National Costume Competition, making her the third Miss Universe winner to do so.

Amelia Vega – Professional Career- After Miss Universe Year

After her years as Miss Universe was over, Amelia continued her activities in the entertainment field.

She started working as a model and appeared in magazines like Oh! Magazine, Cosmopolitan, InStyle, Harper’s Bazaar, Glamour, and more.

After all her work as Miss Universe came to an end, Amelia worked as a host on various television shows. Following that, she made her movie debut in the 2005’s film, The Lost City, directed by Andy Garcia.

Moreover, in 2010, the former beauty queen became the host of the Mexican reality shows Segunda Oportunidad, alongside Rafael Araneda. Later in April, she released her single titled “Pasa Un Segundito” on iTunes.

Just within two days, the song topped the Latin chart. The significant achievement was followed by her complete album “Agua Dulce.” The Dominican promoted her music in many countries like Ecuador, Panama, Puerto Rico, etc.

Along with her singing career, Vega also owns  two boutiques in Miami called “Essence By Amelia Vega.” Also, Amelia was on the cover of the only book by Miss Universe Organization called “Universal Beauty.”
