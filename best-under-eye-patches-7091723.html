The 12 Best Under-Eye Patches of 2024

Fun fact: the skin around your eyes is the thinnest on your entire body. Not-so-fun fact:  that also makes it the first place to show signs of aging or the consequences of not getting your full, recommended eight hours of sleep. (Dark circles, we're looking at you.)

Taking care of your under-eyes is nothing new in the skincare world. We have beauty products, gadgets, and gizmos aplenty to help target some of the trickiest conditions (see aforementioned dark circles). A skincare routine is not complete without a trusty eye cream, but if you're looking to take your under-eye care up a notch, consider adding one of our expert-endorsed patches to your roster. 

To find the best under-eye patches on the shelves, we reviewed countless products in the category and asked dermatologists for their top picks for dry skin, dark circles, puffiness, fine lines, and wrinkles.

Read on for the best under-eye patches to boost radiance and smooth that delicate under-eye area.

    How to Pick the Right Under-Eye Patches

    Similar to choosing the right face mask, the most important part about picking an under-eye patch is to think about your skin concerns and what ingredients work best to fight those things. 

    • For Dry Skin: Look for ingredients like hyaluronic acid, squalane, and vitamin E. Our top pick for hydration is the Joanna Vargas Bright Eye Firming Mask which contains hyaluronic acid and allantoin.
    • For Dark Circles: Look for ingredients like vitamin C, Kakadu plum, and licorice root extract. Our top pick for dark circles is the Pacifica Eye Bright Under-Eye Vitamin C Spot Serum which contains radiance-boosting vitamin C and kelp.
    • For Fine Lines and Wrinkles: Look for ingredients like retinol, collagen, and hyaluronic acid. For fine lines and wrinkles, we love the retinol-infused Dr. Zion x Murad Retinol Youth Renewal Eye Masks.
    • For Puffiness: Look for ingredients like caffeine, allantoin, and niacinamide. Our top pick for puffiness, Peter Thomas Roth Cucumber De-Tox Hydra-Gel Eye Patches, contains caffeine and allantoin.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    • What is the purpose of under-eye patches?

      “An under-eye patch should hydrate the skin and support the skin barrier to help the skin look more plump, smooth, and healthy,” says Dr. King, “It can also contain ingredients to help address other under-eye concerns, like puffiness, dark circles, and fine lines.”


      They function as a way to hold product onto the skin and let it sink in for a prolonged period of time as opposed to applying a serum or moisturizer outright which tends to evaporate from the skin if it is water-based.

    • How often should you use under-eye patches?

      The best practice is to follow the instructions on the packaging. For most patches, that is one to two times per week (though this differs from product to product) — not unlike sheet masks. When the patch contains stronger active ingredients like retinol, consider doing a patch test first to see if your skin can tolerate it before jumping in.

    • How long should you wear an under-eye patch?

      While some social media beauty gurus suggest keeping under-eye patches on all night, our team cautions against it. Most patches are made to be worn for 10-20 minutes to allow the ingredients to fully absorb because that is all it takes to get the full benefits. In addition, the under-eye area is extremely sensitive. Leaving a patch on for too long can exacerbate under-eye issues like puffiness and discoloration. Instead, stick to the instructions on the packaging.
