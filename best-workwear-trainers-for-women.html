15 Best Workwear Trainers For Women | The Sun UK

IMMACULATE dressing for work no longer means tights, pencil skirts and stiletto heels. We're talking neat, well-thought-out looks that don't skimp on comfort.

The best workwear trainers for women can actually look more chic than brogues or loafers, as long as you keep them squeaky clean and select versatile designs that you can wear day-in-day-out.

Ever since we got used to working from home, there's no way we're turning away from comfy footwear for the office.

Time to find yourself a pair of the best workwear trainers for women.

    What are the best workwear trainers for women?

    When deciding on what the best workwear trainers are for you, think about your work's dress code and whether you're going to be on your feet all day.

    If the answer to the second question is yes, then you need to make sure the trainers that you choose are comfortable before anything else.

    Creative jobs such as in marketing or fashion often have loose dress codes, so it means that you can have fun with your footwear choices!

    Overall, though, we recommend opting for all black or all white styles to ensure that your outfit looks as neat and clean-cut as possible for work.

    Where to buy workwear trainers for women

    Most footwear shops online and on the high-street stock a range of trainers that are suitable for work, especially now that the term 'office attire' is really ambiguous.

    We recommend hitting up ASOS, Office, Schuh, John Lewis and Amazon to choose from a variety of workwear trainers, ranging from low-end to high-end.
