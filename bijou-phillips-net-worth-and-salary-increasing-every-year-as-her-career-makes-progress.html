Bijou Phillips' net worth and salary increasing every year as her career makes progress

American model and actress Bijou Philips is a rare beauty. She has a glorious net worth of $20 million. You will also be amazed to know that the ‘Raising Hope’ star Phillips earns $2,400,000 as an annual salary. Isn’t that huge? Well, let’s find out more.?


Bijou Phillips house in Los Angeles

Bijou Phillips along with her husband Danny Masterson lives in a huge mansion in Los Angeles, California. The Hollyridge Drive situated house size is 4,323 square feet. It has 5 bedroom, 4 bathrooms and other extra features such as pool, spa, garden, tennis courts, etc. In 2007, its sale price was $2,995,000 now you can evaluate how much it has increased possibly.

Bijou Philips' expensive rock

Phillips’ wedding ring cannot be unnoticed. It is really huge and gorgeous. Even though Phillips doesn’t show off her ring much, it gets seen in some of her Instagram posts. It is really a beautiful piece which must be very expensive.

Bijou Phillips' glorious career

Phillips is an Equestrienne sports champ. Later, she developed an interest in modeling. She started it at a very young age of 13.As a model, Phillips has appeared on several magazine covers. In fact, she became the youngest person to appear on the cover of ‘Italian Vogue’ and ‘Interview’ magazine. Later, she could also be seen on Calvin Klein’s advertisements. Till date, she has appeared on other magazine covers such as Playboy, Missbehave, and Stuff. In 1999, she realized that she did not just want to be a model but an actress. During an interview, she said: "It was like, I wanted to go swimming in the ocean, but I was jumping up and down in a puddle."

Thus she entered in the movie industry. She appeared in ‘Black and White’ as Charlie in 1999. She was much adored by fans and critics then, due to this, she was able to bag more movies. She also appeared in ‘Almost Famous’ along with Kate Hudson. Besides, she has also appeared in a number of movies such as ‘Tart’, ‘Bully’, ‘The Door in the Floor’, ‘Hotel Chelsea’, ‘Havoc’, ‘Choke’ and ‘Dark Secrets’. Besides, being a model and actress Phillips is also a vocalist. However, she did not prioritize it much. She has also appeared alongside her husband Danny Masterson in an episode of ‘Fox’s Raising Hope’.

Thus we know now how Phillips earned her net worth of $20 million. It can be seen that she has worked really hard for it. However, it does not look like it’s going to stop there. As her career is improving gradually, it is surely going to take her net worth and salary to a higher level.
