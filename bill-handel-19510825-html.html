Bill Handel (Lawyer) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

William Wolf Handel is the director and founder of the Alternative Parenting Center, a licensed lawyer and radio officer in Los Angeles, California. Handel currently hosts two radio shows on the AM 640 KFI in Los Angeles, California. The first is KFI's local morning driving program, in which he comments on current events. The show is one of the highest rated morning shows on the Los Angeles radio market, with over one million listeners. In addition, he hosts a weekend legal consulting program called Handel on the Law, which premiered in 1985 and powered by Premiere Radio Networks, a co-owner of iHeartMedia, Inc.'s KFI. Handel on The Law is listened to on 150 stations in the US His law program is currently his longest radio show to date. Both Handel's programs were broadcast across the United States on American Talk 158 on XM Satellite Radio. In 2008, Handel broadcasts were discontinued by XM Radio when it merged with Sirius Satellite Radio.
