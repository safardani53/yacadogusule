Billboard Music Awards 2016: List of Winners!

The top names in music and pop culture came together for a night of chart-topping craziness Sunday at the 2015 Billboard Music Awards.

Without question, Britney Spears was the star of the night.

The pop singer’s return to early 2000s hotness made the most headlines – and deservedly so – in an event designed to produce them.

But who actually took home the top prizes Sunday?

Not Madonna’s Prince tribute, that’s for sure. (Sorry.) Peep the complete list of winners from the 2016 Billboard Music Awards below:

Top Artist: Adele — WINNER

Billboard Chart Achievement Award (Fan-Voted): Rihanna — WINNER

Top R&B Song: The Weeknd, “The Hills” — WINNER

Top Billboard 200 Album: Adele, 25 — WINNER

Top Male Artist: Justin Bieber — WINNER

Top Country Song: Thomas Rhett, “Die A Happy Man” — WINNER

Top Hot 100 Song: Wiz Khalifa ft. Charlie Puth, “See You Again” — WINNER
