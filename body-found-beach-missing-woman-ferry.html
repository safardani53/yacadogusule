Body found on beach in search for missing woman, 48, who fell overboard on ferry to Portsmouth

THE body of a missing 48-year-old woman has been found washed up on a beach after she fell overboard from a ferry.

Ebla Yusuf vanished on July 22 after she boarded the Commodore Clipper for a crossing between Portsmouth and the Channel Islands.

But cops have now confirmed that Ebla's body was found on a beach in Seaford in East Sussex on July 29.

When the 48-year-old failed to disembark at Guernsey, a major search and rescue operation was launched.

This involved aircraft from the UK and France, as well as Channel Islands Air Search, before the tragic find was made.

A spokesperson for the Hampshire and isle of Wight Constabulary said: "It has now been confirmed this was the body of Ebla Yusuf.
