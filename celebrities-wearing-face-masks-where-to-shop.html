21 Celebrities Wearing Face Masks and Where to Shop Them

As we battle the ongoing coronavirus pandemic, the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) continues to recommend that all Americans wear a face covering in public — and celebrities are, by no means, exempt from this protocol. In fact, many are sharing face mask selfies on social media to remind their followers how important wearing one is to slow the spread of the virus; Jennifer Aniston, Jessica Alba, Reese Witherspoon, and Jennifer Hudson are just a few A-listers to raise awareness.

Other stars let their personal style shine through as they abide by the CDC's guidelines. Irina Shayk was spotted walking around in a zebra-print disposable option, Jennifer Lopez opted for a houndstooth bedazzled covering, and Chrissy Teigen shared a photo on Instagram wearing a silky leopard print mask. A handful of stars — including Ariana Grande, Hailey Bieber, and Katie Holmes — have even been seen wearing the same black disposable mask from Evolvetogether.

Since face coverings will continue to be essential for the foreseeable future, we tracked down the face masks celebrities are wearing. Keep scrolling to learn where you can shop their favorites.

Irina Shayk

The supermodel is just one of many Hollywood A-listers who have worn protective essentials from Maskc. The brand has quickly become a go-to among celebrities for its disposable face masks that are printed with bright colors and trendy patterns. Some other famous fans include Lucy Hale, Mandy Moore, Cara Delevingne, and Alessandra Ambrosio. Shayk opted for the brand's zebra print masks, which you can get for 22 percent off thanks to Maskc's secret sale. It's not being advertised on the website, but if you enter the code DOUBLEMASK at checkout, you'll get the discount.

Jennifer Lopez

It's no secret that J.Lo loves anything sparkly — so of course, she has quite a few protective essentials decked out in rhinestones, including the Noli Yoga Crystal Houndstooth face mask. She's been spotted wearing it on numerous occasions with everything from workout gear to matching sweatsuits. Made from soft material, the mask features a bendable nose wire, adjustable ear loops, and a built-in filter pocket for additional protection. It normally costs $50, but for a limited time, you can score it for 74 percent off — knocking the price down to just $18 per bedazzled mask. Lopez was also previously seen wearing a white sequin face mask from Katie May (dubbed the Disco Ball mask), as well as three different rhinestone-embellished coverings from MasQd. (We weren't kidding about her love of sparkles.)

Chrissy Teigen

Buy It! Slip Pure Silk Adult Pleated Face Covering in Rose Leopard, $39; nordstrom.com; amazon.com

The Cravings cookbook author recently shared a photo on Instagram of her wearing a rose-colored, leopard print face mask from Slip. Made from the same 100 percent mulberry silk material used in the brand's pillowcases, eye masks, and hair scrunchies, the masks are designed to be more delicate against your skin than cotton options, since the fabric is naturally hypoallergenic and breathable. For $39, you can get Teigen's exact mask from Amazon or Nordstrom.

Ariana Grande

Buy It Evolvetogether Milan 30 Face Masks, $35.97; evolvetogether.com

Ariana Grande shared a selfie on Instagram wearing a mask from the brand so many other celebs are wearing right now: Evolvetogether. Its disposable face masks are some of the only medical-grade masks available to consumers. Each one features a water-resistant outer layer that blocks airborne contaminants, a melt-blown center that filters out bacteria and particles, and a moisture-absorbing inner layer that makes them breathable. You can grab a set of seven for just under $9, which is available to ship immediately, or a set of 30 for $35.97.

Kate Middleton

Buy It! Amaia Adult Reusable Cotton Face Mask - Pink Pepper, $17.90; amaiakids.com

Floral face masks were trending less than 24 hours after the Duchess of Cambridge stepped out wearing one from the London-based brand Amaia Kids. Her exact mask has been restocked, but we were also inspired to seek out similar versions in the pretty pink print. Some of our favorites include this nearly identical option from Etsy for $9 and this cute wildflower mask that's $13 on Amazon.

Selma Blair

Selma Blair has been spotted wearing a floral face mask (like Kate Middleton!) from online printing service Vistaprint. The brand's masks are designed with a three-dimensional chin structure, nose bridge, and adjustable ear straps to ensure they fit every face and stay secure for as long as you need. Each one is made from a triple layer of polyester fiber, spandex, and a hypoallergenic cotton lining that's soft on the skin. Blair's floral face mask is still available for $18 and should arrive within one week of purchase. You can also buy three or more Vistaprint face masks and get 25 percent off with the code FAST25.

Katie Holmes

Katie Holmes and her boyfriend Emilio Vitolo Jr. have been non-stop matching in the Evolvetogether masks Ariana Grande has also worn. But when she's out alone, Holmes has been sporting a reusable option from Baublebar. Her pink gingham print mask is made from two layers of cotton fabric that feel soft and breathable, and it comes with ear loops that can be adjusted by sliding the attached beads for a more secure and comfortable fit. It's available in a two-pack (which includes a floral embroidered version) for just $12, and for a limited time, you can score two mask sets for the price of one.

Alessandra Ambrosio

The supermodel has been hitting the streets wearing various patterned face masks over the past year. But the one that appears to be her favorite is a cheetah print mask from Sanctuary, a Los Angeles-based clothing company. Before their split, Ben Affleck and former girlfriend Ana de Armas were also spotted wearing masks from this brand around his neighborhood. The masks are made from a double layer of 100 percent cotton muslin and feature a built-in nose wire and filter. Sanctuary is selling packs of five animal print masks (including Ambrosio's exact one) for $28, but there are only a few left in stock. Shoppers can also snag a deal on this family bundle that comes with five adult masks and five kids' masks for $42.

Hailey Bieber

Hailey Bieber has been seen wearing a slew of different reusable and disposable face masks — one of our favorites being her blue leopard print mask from Los Angeles-based contemporary clothing company Michael Stars. The brand's masks are made from 100 percent cotton fabric lined by a black contrast binding. Each one features soft, elastic ear loops and a double layer of interfacing non-woven fabric that acts as a filter. You can shop a pack of two leopard masks for $38.

Jessica Alba

Buy It! Honest Adult Cotton Face Masks 2-Pack, $24.99; honest.com

Jessica Alba's company Honest is selling reusable cloth face masks for adults and kids. So, of course, Alba has been wearing different ones from her brand, including a colorful rose mask and a cactus print mask, which can be seen in a photo posted to her Instagram. Made from a soft, 100 percent cotton material, each Honest face mask features adjustable silicone sliders on the stretchy ear loops and a bendable nose piece for a more secure fit. Plus, there's an interior lining that's designed to fit a filter. Each two-pack costs $25 and comes with one playful pattern and one coordinating solid color.

Jennifer Aniston

Buy It! Wolford Care Face Covering, $35; shopbop.com; zappos.com

On her social media accounts, Jennifer Aniston has been an advocate for face masks. At the end of June, she shared a photo wearing an all-black mask from Wolford, the same one Madam Vice President Kamala Harris regularly sported on the campaign trail. It's made from a double layer of breathable polyester fabric that has a water-repellant finish and adaptable nose wires for a more contoured fit. The Wolford mask is available on the brand's website as well as Zappos and Shopbop.

Tracee Ellis Ross

Buy It! Copper Line Copper Mask - Reusable Washable Protective Face Mask, $33.99; amazon.com

The Black-ish star posted a selfie on Instagram of herself wearing a reusable copper-infused face mask that's available on Amazon for $34. It's made from six-ply layers, including a patented nano-ionized copper fabric, and is said to be 99 percent effective, even after 30 washes. The mask features an air zone pocket that offers more room to breathe and a nose pad that helps prevent glasses from fogging.

Nina Dobrev

The actress has been spotted wearing a colorful face mask from Summersalt on numerous occasions — both in public and on Instagram. The brand, known for its super flattering one-piece swimsuits, launched machine-washable masks made from three layers of recycled, 100 percent cotton material. For extra comfort, they have beads on the ear loops that can easily be adjusted to alleviate painful tugging on your ears. The Summersalt face masks are so popular, that they've already sold out twice — but luckily, they're in stock now. Grab a pack of three for $32 before they're gone again.

Jennifer Hudson

The singer shared on Instagram back in April 2020 that she purchased her face mask from Etsy seller Kristian Kouture Shop. The handmade masks are constructed using three layers of breathable cotton material with elastic ear loops and a filter pocket (filter not included). One mask is available for $15, and you can choose from 11 different colors or be surprised by a random fun print. If you want to support a small business like Hudson, there are now millions of face masks available from Etsy sellers.

Busy Philipps

Busy Philipps has shared some of her favorite masks on Instagram, but there's one brand she's been particularly fond of called Lele Sadoughi. "Her masks are kinda my favs — they fit really well and don't make me feel claustrophobic and also you can put a filter in them (which I do)," she captioned a photo of her wearing a matching cheetah print mask and headband in July. Lele Sadoughi is known for its hair accessories that are loved by celebs and influencers alike, so it makes sense that the company is creating face masks to match its knotted headbands. The masks (and headbands, for that matter) are made from soft, stretchy cotton material. You can order Philipps' favorite for $35 as well as any of the other Lele Sadoughi face masks packs (also available in kids' sizes).

Kourtney Kardashian

Buy It! Skims Seamless Face Mask, $8; skims.com; nordstrom.com

The Keeping Up with the Kardashians star posted a selfie wearing her Skims face mask from sister Kim Kardashian's shapewear line. The ultra-soft, breathable masks are made from 97 percent nylon and 3 percent spandex and come in five tonal colors, just like the brand's undergarments. They've been so popular that they've sold out multiple times on the Skims website. But the masks recently got restocked and are now available on Nordstrom.

Storm Reid

Back in August, the Wrinkle in Time actress accepted Tory Burch's viral challenge and shared a selfie on Instagram wearing a blue face mask from Rothy's, known as The Mask 1.0. It's knit from the company's trademarked recycled polyester (rPET) thread that's used to make its flats, flip-flops, and handbags. The masks have elastic ear straps and microfiber swatches on the inside for added comfort. Plus, each one features Rothy's signature blue halo marking along the nose bridge to match your pointed-toe flats. Reid's face mask has been waitlisted for quite some time, but it's finally available again and comes in a pack of two for $25.

Meghan Markle

Buy It! Royal Jelly Harlem Adult Mask in Blue Seersucker, $15; royalharlemjelly.com

Meghan Markle's choice of face mask comes from Royal Harlem Jelly, a small, Black-owned business run by mother-daughter duo Teta and Maya Gorgoni. According to the brand's website, it purchases many of its materials from African vendors and many of its manufacturers are African-born tailors who produce the products in the United States. Meghan's blue-and-white striped mask is still available in both adult and kids' sizes for $15. When these inevitably sell out, you can shop this best-selling seersucker mask from Etsy for the same price, or grab this similar style from Amazon for $7.

Vanessa Hudgens

The actress shared a selfie on Instagram wearing a face mask from MasQd, the female- and minority-owned small business whose protective gear has become another favorite among Hollywood A-listers. The brand's adult and kids' masks are made from a soft, certified antimicrobial material that's even more comfortable than a regular covering thanks to its non-elastic ear loops. Each one comes with a metal nose piece that can be adjusted for a better fit around the face. Hudgens' gold butterfly print mask is currently out of stock, but there are plenty of other fun patterns to choose from.

Hilary Duff

Buy It! Werkshop Roses Face Mask, $26; werkshop.com

Hilary Duff is just one of the dozens of celebrities to step out in a protective face covering from Werkshop. Joe Jonas, Sophie Turner, Charlize Theron, Laura Dern, and Olivia Wilde have also worn the brand's face masks. Duff's rose print version is made from two thick layers of Werkshop's comfy athleisure fabric, with one serving as a pocket for mask filters. It also has soft, elastic edges that hug the face for a snug fit. The Werkshop masks come in a variety of fun prints, and one mask retails for $26.

Millicent Simmonds

The Quiet Place actress partnered with fair-trade fashion brand, Rafi Nova, to design the Millie Smile Mask, a custom transparent mask that restores visual cues like lip-reading and facial expressions to communication. Net proceeds from the masks are being donated to the non-profit organization Deaf Women of Color and Loving Hands and Hearts based in Texas, which helps parents of children who are deaf. Designed by both Millie and a speech-language pathology clinical fellow, the mask features a wide transparent panel and custom floral print.
