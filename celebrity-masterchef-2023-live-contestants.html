Who won Celebrity MasterChef 2022?

With the return of Celebrity MasterChef just hours away, let’s look back on last year’s series.

After five intense weeks of heats, semi-finals, and the final week of culinary challenges, one celeb managed to beat all competitors in the kitchen, and was awarded the trophy by judges Gregg Wallace and John Torode.

And after all three finalists cooked their hearts out is was Lisa Snowdon who walked off as Celebrity MasterChef champion.

MasterChef 2024 applications are open

Could you be the next MasterChef? Here’s how to apply for the 2024 series.

You can apply to appear on next year’s MasterChef by heading over to the BBC MasterChef’s take part page.

Applications are open until August 28, 2023, until 11:59pm.

The BBC has stated that if you require assistance with your online application, you can email info.masterchef@shine.tv.

You must complete a profile, respond to a few questions, accept the terms and conditions, and submit any necessary media in order to make the application.

What else has James Buckley done since leaving The Inbetweeners?

James has continued to act since ending his run on the E4 show, and has remained mainly in the comedy sector.

He played a young Del Boy in the short-lived Only Fools And Horses prequel, Rock And Chips.

He starred as Brian Weaver in fantasy-comedy Zapped from 2016-2018, and appeared in US comedy I Feel Bad in 2018.

From 2017 until 2019, James played Brian Fitzpatrick in sitcom White Gold, reuniting with former Inbetweeners co-star Joe Thomas.

Who is MasterChef The Professionals judge Anna Haugh?

Anna Haugh was born on November 6, 1980 in Dublin, Ireland.

She is best known for being an Irish chef, restaurateur and TV personality.

Anna owns a restaurant called Myrtle located in Chelsea.

The posh restaurant offers a range of modern European cuisines prepared with an Irish influence.

Which movies has Dani Dyer been in?

Dani has quite a few film credits to her name and winning Love Island only added to this.

She landed herself the role of Lucy in movie Heckle.

In the horror flick she plays a rebellious teenager who sneaks off into the woods with her boyfriend at a party to smoke weed and ‘get up to no good’.

She also plays Nancy Francis in 031, another horror movie.

Before reality TV, Dani started off with small parts in the movies The Other Half, Doghouse, Run for Your Wide and Vendetta.

Later, she plated Lauren in We Still Kill the Old Way in 2014 alongside impressive actors like Ian Ogilvy, Alison Doody and Christopher Ellison.

Since then, she has played Joss Blake in Age of Kill (2015) and Elizabeth in Bonded by Blood 2 (2017).
