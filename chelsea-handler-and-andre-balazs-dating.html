Chelsea Handler and Andre Balazs: Dating!

Let’s hope 50 Cent doesn’t read this or he might pop a cap in something.

Chelsea Handler has gone public with hotelier Andre Balazs, according to Us. She and her alleged beau of four months stepped out together in NYC.

An unusually serious Handler also opened up about her man during a promotional appearance for her new book on Tuesday’s Piers Morgan Tonight.

“I’m seeing someone now, but,” she demurred, “I have spent so much time exposing my personal life to everybody that I finally am in a situation where I feel like it’s disrespectful to another person,” she said of Balazs.

“I’m not being coy. We know what’s happening,” added Handler, whose exes include 50, Animal Planet star Dave Salmoni and Comcast CEO Ted Harbert.

“I’ve just learned to respect somebody else’s privacy. It’s not about me, because obviously I would like to make fun of him as much as possible.”

“But he has kids, so I have to be nice. I have very strong feelings.”

Sounds like love to us!
