Chuck Hagel Hollywood Life

Bio:
Chuck Hagel (born Charles Timothy Hagel on Oct. 4, 1946 in North Platte, Nebraska) has done everything from serving in the army, to creating a mobile phone service carrier. In 1996, Chuck became the first Republican senator of Nebraska in 24 years. In 2008, he retired from the U.S. Senate and wrote a book called America: Our Next Chapter: Tough Questions, Straight Answers, in which he suggested the country adopt an independent leadership.
