Who is Clarissa Molina:

Clarissa Molina is a famous Pageant Contestant. She was born on September 23, 1991 and her birthplace is Santiago, Dominican Republic. Clarissa is also well known as, Famous for winning the reality television-based beauty pageant Nuestra Belleza Latina in 2016 and for finishing as runner-up for the same competition in 2015. She is also notable as the winner of the 2012 Miss Dominican Republic Tourism International and 2013 Miss Latina New Jersey USA pageants.

Clarissa is originated from Dominican Republic. She competed against fellow model Barbara Turbay in Nuestra Belleza Latina 2016.
