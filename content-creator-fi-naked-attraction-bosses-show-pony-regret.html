I was on Naked Attraction - show bosses made me feel like a 'show pony'

A CONTESTANT on Naked Attraction has slammed the show as she opened up on her regrets on taking part.

Content creator Fi was the first contestant to appear during an episode of the Channel 4 show that aired in October.

The Twitch streamer appeared in the studio with host Anna Richardson, 52, as she was looking for love.

However, the internet personality has now taken to social media in a new video on TikTok, where she spoke about her bad experience on Naked Attraction.

She accused producers of forcing her to wear cosplay on set even though she didn't want to.

The gamer also feels the show portrayed her in a certain way as they cut out various positive comments in the edit, and she says producers were not 'considerate' to her sexuality.

Fi explained: "I was on Naked Attraction, and the only attraction has been bad attraction.

"So why did I do the show? To be honest, for the experience more than anything, and it has brought a lot of pain.

"Do I regret it? Yes. The main reason why I regret it was because of how the show portrayed me.

"So my main thing was [i was looking for] someone out of the cosplay ... they wanted me to wear a wig on the actual show and my elf ears.

"I was really upset about this so they said 'fine, just the elf ears.' To be honest, I felt like nothing more than a show pony.

"As well as the fact that they kept making me, so like the reasons I gave for getting rid of a certain colour palette, I was made to change my answers.

"Because I am pansexual, so physical attraction isn't really a thing for me and I don't think they were really considering of that were giving my answers.

"Another thing when I went on the show, I wanted to make a big deal of stretch marks.

"There was one girl on there, [she] had absolutely beautiful body, had beautiful stretch marks and everything, because stretch marks are really natural and they should be embraced.

"They cut everything I said about that kind of stuff out of the show.

"I have watched the whole episode and I am disgusted by it, I'm disgusted by the whole experience, I literally was just a show pony."

The former contestant also claims that she has received trolling on various platforms as a result of her appearance on the show.

She exclaimed: "Besides that, I've had people come into my live-streams, DMs and everything, just to tell me how disgusting my body and my stretch marks are.

"That they should have have trigger warnings because I have scars. And I have been made to feel more disgusting in my life.
