I make the ultimate cheese and ham toastie in my air fryer with my 85p hack &amp; it tastes as good as C

A FOOD fan has revealed that she makes the ultimate cheese and ham toastie using her air fryer.

Lauren, who is known on social media as @laurenscravings, has amassed 28.8k followers and 341.1k likes on TikTok, where she regularly shares her food tips and tricks.

The Scottish woman, who lives in Glasgow, recently took to the video sharing platform to share how she makes her very own version of Costa’s cheese and ham toastie in an air fryer.

So if you’re a fan of Costa’s food and want to know how to make your own at home, it’s time to start making notes. 

And according to this food whizz, she has a simple secret to get the best tasting toastie - béchamel sauce. 

She said: “I’ve completed the year of the sandwich with a Costa ham and cheese toastie.

“Just using shop bought béchamel for this one, it’s Dolmio creamy lasagne sauce.

“Spread that on both sides of the bread, add some gammon and then a mix of mozzarella and cheddar cheese.

“Close it over and top it with more creamy lasagne sauce and cheese on top and that’s how you achieve that crispy, cheesy finish.

“Into the air fryer for ten minutes at 180 degrees celsius.
