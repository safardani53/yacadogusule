A DAD who fathered six kids with his daughter tricked her into drinking urine and gave her sex tips by pretending to be psychic, a court heard.

The 'psychic' sent her emails telling her drinking urine was how witches drew power over their lovers, the daughter said in a pre-recorded police interview.

The interview was played to a jury today during the third day of an incest trial at Swansea Crown Court.

She said "Emilia" gave her advice on performing sex acts in a series of emails spanning almost 20 years.

The daughter was 15 at the time she was told to drink the urine and had recently given birth to their first child.

She said: "I was gullible and I drank it."

The father, who cannot be named to protect the identity of his alleged victims, bought them a pair of rings on eBay and told her the seller "Emilia" had called to offer her services as a psychic "for free", the daughter said.

In the interview, the daughter said: “The psychic sent messages saying witches drink urine to make them have control over their men.”

When the police officer asked if she had drunk the urine, the daughter replied: “Yes.”

"Emilia" deterred the daughter, now aged 37, from starting relationships with other men and regularly gave her tips on how to have better sex with her father, the court heard.

She said: "I’d get emails from Emilia almost every day.

“The emails were sexual from very early on. One told me that if my fingers felt like they were burning, I have got to give a blow job.”

At the time, she said she was suffering from nerve damage in her fingers, and had complained to her father of the pain before receiving the email.

The daughter said: “I still did not link the two."

Another email from the"psychic" advised her on how to have sex.

The daughter said the email had read: “Head down, bum up. I just thought she was giving advice.

“She has got her reasons, I thought. She’s a psychic. She’s trying to help me.

“It became routine. It’s something I had always known and done.”

In the interview, the daughter, said she first met her father when she was aged 13 and living with her mother.

After the initial meeting in Wales, she began spending weekends at her father’s house in England.

On one weekend visit he told her he was not her real dad, the court heard.

He said she had been conceived while he was in prison and put his name on her birth certificate.

During the weekend visits he allegedly plied the teenager with alcohol and cigarettes and then began touching her sexually.

He told her they were “soul mates”, the daughter said.

When she was 14, she became pregnant after having sex with her father on a sofa bed while his partner slept in an upstairs bedroom, the daughter said said.

She said in the interview: "He told me not to tell anybody I was pregnant."

When it her baby bump became obvious, she said the father told her to tell people she had got pregnant “with a boy I was seeing after school”.

The father denies 36 counts of rape and one count of assault by penetration.
