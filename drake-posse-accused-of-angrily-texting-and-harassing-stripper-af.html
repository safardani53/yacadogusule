Drake, Posse Accused of &quot;Angrily&quot; Texting &amp; Harassing Stripper After Consensual Sex

Stripper Jhonni Blaze claims Drake’s camp threatened her after she had sex with the rapper recently, and Houston police are reportedly investigating.

The official document only identifies the male party under investigation as a “possible celebrity,” but TMZ confirms it’s Drizzy who she’s accusing. 

Jhonni Blaze, which is probably not her real name, but is an awesome one for a stripper if it is, has told the police that she and Drake had sex once.

One time too many, the stripper (not pictured above, that’s Nicki Minaj dancing for Drake in her “Anaconda” video) believes after what transpired.

The star believed, or so she claims, that she was going to blab about this to the media, and began “angrily” texting her as a result of this belief.

Why he was so sure of this, she doesn’t say, but she claims that several people from Drake’s camp then came to her house and banged on her door.

 

Among other choice comments, they threatened her life.

Blaze went to the police because she was afraid but admits that she’s not sure if she wants to follow through and press charges against anyone.

Police, meanwhile, say they want to talk to the “celebrity,” and sources close to Drake commented briefly to say these allegations are not true at all.

Stay tuned to find out if the authorities agree.
