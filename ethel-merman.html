Who is Ethel Merman:

Ethel Merman is a famous Stage Actress. She was born on January 16, 1908 and her birthplace is Astoria, NY. Ethel is also well known as, Singer and actress who was in many musicals and sang “There’s No Business like Show Business,” as well as hits like “You’re the Top” and “Anything Goes.”

Ethel is originated from United States. She was greatly inspired by performers such as Fanny Brice who she saw in the Vaudeville show at the Palace Theatre on Friday nights with her family.
