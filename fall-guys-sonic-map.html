Fall Guys Sonic crossover map discovered

THE full Fall Guys and Sonic crossover map has been found in the game files.

Bean Hill Zone, a level apparently based on ‘Hoopsy Daisy’, was initially discovered earlier this month following a datamine.

Fans believed that the level would be released in August when other Sonic-themed costumes are set to launch.

The collaboration was announced in June, when players will be given the chance to equip their beans with Sonic, Tails, or Knuckles costumes. 

However, no other Sonic themed levels, items, or events have been officially announced to date.

Twitter user @FGPancake, who found the initial screenshot, has now uncovered the entire map and has even recorded some gameplay footage.

More in Fall Guys

While the images seem legitimate, there is no way to confirm the authenticity of the leaks.

It could be that the user simply invented the level from scratch.

If the leak is real, it will be covered with a number of bumpers from the Sonic games, as well as rings and loops.

The new footage shows a map that is much larger than the one originally imagined from the screenshot.

Most read in Gaming

Unlike ‘Hoopsy Daisy’ the rings are smaller and there are many more of them to collect.

It seems the new level will be based around collecting a large number of rings with obstacles in your path.

The level is surrounded by a platform with a moving floor, and tubes connect upper and lower portions of the zone.

Music can be heard in the leak but it appears to be closer to Fall Guys Music than anything from Sonic. 

Other Fall Guys players have added to the validity of the claim.

Several players have mentioned that they have seen glimpses of the ‘Bean Hill Zone’ level screen during loading. 

One commenter called the unannounced level a “poorly kept secret”.
