My favorite lazy girl meal ever takes 10 minutes to make - you only need 3 ingredients and it's heal

A NUTRITIONIST has revealed her favorite lazy girl meal that is super-healthy and uses only three ingredients.

Even better, this recipe required no prep or chopping and took only 10 minutes to make.

This delicious dish idea came from Maty Harrington.

She posts her healthy food plans on her TikTok to her 552,000 followers, as Nutritiously Easy (@nutritiouslyeasy).

“This is my favorite lazy girl meal ever to make. It’s so healthy," she began her post.

There were so many benefits to this recipe other than nutritional ones: “Mostly [has] three ingredients, and there’s zero chopping," she said.

First, she started cooking some ground beef but explained ground turkey or chicken could also be used.

When this was 80 percent cooked through, she added the other two elements of this meal.

“You’re going to take a coleslaw packet and a broccoli slaw packet – got these from Whole Foods, but also sold at Trader Joe and every other grocery store.

“I’m just going to add in half of each. If you’re really lazy you can do just one of these but I like the variation of both," she explained.

She was generous with her sauce additions, mixing in "fish sauce, coconut aminos, soy sauce, and some honey."

For good measure, she added some extra spice with a generous dollop of Sriracha.

“Let [the ingredients] soak in the sauce. It’s literally so good,” she said of the finished dish.

There were a lot of whetted appetites to her post, with over 16,000 liking her meal idea.

One commenter was grateful for the recipe.

“Thank you for this. I’d been struggling to find yummy quick dinners when I’m too tired to cook. My husband who does not like cabbage, loved it.”

Another said: “I just did the same exact thing for dinner. And I only added soy sauce and some seasonings. So freaking good."
