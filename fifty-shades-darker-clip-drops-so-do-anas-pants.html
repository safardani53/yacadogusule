Fifty Shades Darker Clip Drops, So Do Ana's Pants

The first official clip from Fifty Shades Darker has dropped, and in it, Ana Steele’s underwear fittingly drops to the floor along with it.

In public, no less. Does that go without saying?

Titled "Date Night," the scene from the film is probably not representative of most of your recent date nights with your significant other.

If we’re wrong about that, well, we’re impressed.

After all, it’s not easy to keep things that hot – or whatever you call the act of taking off your underwear in a fancy restaurant after dinner.

Which is precisely what Jamie Dornan’s Christian Grey requests of his girlfriend, Dakota Johnson’s Ana, in the 50 Shades of Grey sequel.

"Take ’em off," he instructs Anastasia.

"Right in here?" Steele asks.

"Right here," he responds.

Obviously, she complies:

Yep. Off they go.

That’s not the end of this sensual endeavor, either. As they leave the restaurant in a crowded elevator, Christian just can’t help himself.

Unable to manage his impulses despite being surrounded by others (seriously, elevator is packed), he reaches up Anastasia’s dress.

Perhaps the individuals surrounding them are all cyborgs, or half-asleep, because somehow no one else seems to notice this behavior.

Set to Taylor Swift and Zayn Malik’s song "I Don’t Wanna Live Forever," the two will make everyone sweat in their seats come February 10.

That’s when the film hits theaters, and if it’s at all like the first installment, it will be critically lampooned and hugely successful commercially.

Seems like a fairly safe bet.
