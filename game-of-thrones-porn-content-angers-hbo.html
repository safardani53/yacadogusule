Game of Thrones Porn Content Angers HBO

Game of Thrones often depicts boobs and bare backsides.

But there are also dragons and murders and political intrigue and family drama.

In other words: the HBO pic is not porn. And the network doesn’t want itself to be associated with porn.

For this reason (and for actual copyright infringement), HBO is taking action against PornHub for after naked scenes from Game of Thrones appeared on the NSFW website.

According to HBO, numerous scenes from Game of Thrones Season 6 were uploaded to the pornographic website, in addition to parodies of porn stars making like characters such as Lord Varys, Cersei Lannister and Daenerys Targaryen.

“HBO is aware of the issue and is in the process of getting material taken down from PornHub,” the premium cable network said in a statement to The Sun on Wednesday.

As you might expect, an endless number of Internet users run Game of Thrones-related searches during the season.

And they especially run searches for Emilia Clarke nude or Lena Headley nude.

Which is ironic, of course, because you can just watch Game of Thrones online or on television to see these actresses nude.

PornHub, meanwhile,  recently released data that said that searches for Game of Thrones were “slightly above average” in the month of April.

However, these searches “shot up in the days before the season premiere” on April 24, peaking at 370 percent.

The website also stated that Clarke was the top search among all of the show’s stars.

Not shocking, is it? She’s The Mother of Dragons and The Desirable Object of Horny Men on the Internet.
