Who is Geoff Bodine:

Geoff Bodine is a famous Race Car Driver. He was born on April 18, 1949 and his birthplace is Chemung, NY. Geoff is also well known as, Eldest of the three NASCAR Bodine brothers, retired in 2012 after a 27-year career that included over 200 top-ten finishes.

Geoff is originated from United States. His younger brothers Brett and Todd Bodine were also extremely successful during their NASCAR careers.
