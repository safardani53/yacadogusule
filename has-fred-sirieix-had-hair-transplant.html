Has Fred Sirieix had a hair transplant?

AS THE suave maître d' of the First Dates restaurant, Fred Sirieix is known for his handsome good looks and charming personality.

Over the years he has changed his hairstyle and now sports much thicker locks, leading fans to wonder if he's had a hair transplant.

Has Fred Sirieix had a hair transplant?

Celebrities are constantly put under pressure to look good.

So it will come as no surprise that some male stars opt for some surgical help when it comes to losing their locks.

However, after some speculation for Fred Sirieix's fans, it has been revealed that he hasn't undergone a hair transplant.

This type of cosmetic surgery is used to target male and female pattern baldness.

Results can permanently change your appearance, but the hairs can take over a year to grow in entirely.

Patients undergoing the surgical procedure are put under local anaesthetic and sedation.

The procedure can be carried out in two separate ways - either a follicular unit transplantation or a follicular unit extraction.

What has Fred said about rumours of his hair transplant?

During his stint on I'm A Celebrity 2023, Fred broke his silence on whether or not he'd had a hair transplant.

During a video, which was posted on the ITV reality show's Instagram account, the contestants were asked a series of quickfire questions, which included: “What’s the funniest thing you’ve read about yourself that wasn’t true?"

Fred's answer was that people wrongly assumed that he wore a wig or had undergone a hair transplant.

“I’ll tell you what it is. It’s that everybody thinks I’ve got a toupee or I went to Turkey to get new hair,” he said. “I just grew it!”

What surgery has Fred Sirieix had?
