STRATFORD

2.00

PRINCETON ROYALE has been knocking on the door and was a good second at Fakenham last time out. That was on the back of hitting the frame in his three previous outings.

He could do with a win and has a capable 7lb claimer on board today, making his mark look attractive.

2.35 - Mullen's star bet (add to your betslip)

CRAFTY ROBERTO ran his best race for a while when second at Plumpton last time out. That form has been franked since and he should appreciate this better ground.

3.10

DEFINATELY VINNIE likes this trip and won with plenty in hand the last time he got some decent ground. He was a good third in the mud at Hereford latest and looks handicapped to go close.

3.40

LITTERALE CI comes here in decent form after a fair third at Wincanton last time out. She has dropped to just 4lb higher than the mark that saw her win at Ludlow before Christmas.

That’s also the last time the ground wasn’t testing and she gets on well with today’s rider.

4.10

SECRET INVESTOR has been found a good opening after finishing second at Wincanton last time out. He tried to make all there and just got a bit tired in the closing stages.

He drops in distance and gets better ground here.

4.45

UPBEAT COBBLER has tumbled down the weights and is almost a stone lighter than her last winning mark.

She should have no problem with this step up in distance and showed promise when second at Huntingdon last time out.

5.15

BILLY MERRIOTT went close in a similar contest to this at Newbury last time out, going down in a photo finish. He can be a bit hit and miss but should appreciate these conditions.
