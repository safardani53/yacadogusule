Twenty-one years into flavor forecasting and McCormick is still uncovering the trends that shake up the way we cook, flavor and eat. Today, four new trends were announced as part of the brand’s Flavor Forecast® 21st Edition — Physiological Eating, Plants Pushing Boundaries, Humble Nosh and Underwater, Under Discovered — and this year, they’re letting astrology guide consumers to their flavor destiny.
