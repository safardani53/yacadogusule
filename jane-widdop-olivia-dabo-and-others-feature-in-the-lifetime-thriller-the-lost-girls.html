Jane Widdop, Olivia DAbo And Others Feature In The Lifetime Thriller The Lost Girls

The Lost Girls is an upcoming thriller that will premiere on Lifetime on Saturday, March 26, 2022, at 8 p.m. ET/PT. The Lifetime film was directed by Julia Verdin. It was originally released under a different title in 2020.

According to Lifetime’s synopsis of the film,

“One adolescent girl strives to reconnect with herself and her family after escaping from a s*x trafficking organization. To save her defenseless companions, she must face her anxieties and, at all costs, assist the authorities in locating her traffickers.”

Jane Widdop, Randall Batinkoff, Olivia d’Abo, Juliette Hanover, and Dylan Sprayberry are among the potential cast members of the upcoming thriller The Lost Girls.

The Lost Girls’ Cast has been revealed for Lifetime’s next thriller

Olivia D’Abo

Olivia D’Abo, a British singer and actress, will play one of the central protagonists in this new Lifetime drama. Conan the Destroyer, Flying, Mission Kill, Into the Fire, Beyond the Stars, The Spirit of ’76, Point of No Return, The Last Good Time, and Clean Slate are among the films in which she has appeared.

She’s also starred in films including Kicking and Screaming, A Texas Funeral; It Had to Be You, A Poor Kid’s Guide to Success, The Devil’s Violinist, Stolen from the Suburbs, and A Christmas Eve Miracle, and more.

Not My Kid, Growing Pains, The Wonder Years, The Legend of Prince Valiant, Star Trek: The Next Generation, Fantasy Island, The Legend of Tarzan, The Twilight Zone, Law & Order: Criminal Intent, Jane the Virgin, and others are among her famous TV appearances.

Jane Widdop

Jane Widdop, a promising actress, will play a major role in the next Lifetime film. Viewers are looking forward to seeing what the young actress offers to this brand new thriller. Murder in the First (2015), Making Moves (2016), The Kicks (2016), Speechless (2017), Fresh Off the Boat (2018), and Yellowjackets are just a few of the notable TV programs in which she has appeared (2021-2022).

She’s also appeared in Bloodline (2013), Jessica Darling’s It List as Hope (2016), Deadly Daughter Switch as Breanne (2020), and other films.

Randall Batinkoff

Randall Batinkoff, an American actor, will play one of the central protagonists in the forthcoming Lifetime thriller The Lost Girls. Streetwalkin’, Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Higher Learning, Walking and Talking, The Peacemaker, As Good as It Gets, Dead Man’s Curve, Let the Devil Wear Black, and others are just a few of the significant films in which he has appeared.

He also appeared in The Last Marshal, Just Sue Me, The Month of August, I Love Your Work, Walking on the Sky, X-Men: First Class, Who Gets the Dog? The Last Champion, and a number of more films. He’s also been in shows including Better Days, Christy, Relativity, Son of the Beach, CSI: Miami, Mind Games, Legends of Tomorrow, Allegiance, and a few others. Juliette Hanover and Dylan Sprayberry are among the other cast members for The Lost Girls.
