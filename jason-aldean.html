Jason Aldean Hollywood Life

Bio:
Jason Aldean (born Jason Aldine Williams in Macon, Georgia on February 28 1977) is an American country music singer. He has one double platinum album, and he has recorded five albums overall. Nine of his singles have reached the number one position on the Hot Country Songs charts.

Best Known For:
Jason Aldean is best known for being a country music singer.

Personal Life:
Jason Aldean married his high school sweetheart Jessica Ussery in 2001. Jason and his wife have two daughters. Their marriage was rocked in the autumn of 2012 when Jason admitted to cheating with former American Idol contestant Brittany Kerr. Jason filed for divorce on April 26 2013, citing irreconcilable differences.
