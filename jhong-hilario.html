Who is Jhong Hilario:

Jhong Hilario is a famous Movie Actor. He was born on August 11, 1976 and his birthplace is Makati, Philippines. Jhong is also well known as, Dancer turned actor best known for his roles in films such as Muro Ami, Bagong Buwan, and The Healing, among others. His television credits include episodes of Maalaala Mo Kaya and Kung Fu Kids.

Jhong is originated from Philippines. He and Vhong Navarro were both in the cast of the 2005 film D’Anothers.
