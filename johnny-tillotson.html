Who is Johnny Tillotson:

Johnny Tillotson is a famous Country Singer. He was born on April 20, 1939 and his birthplace is Jacksonville, FL. Johnny is also well known as, Country and pop artist who scored top five singles with “Poetry in Motion” and “It Keeps Right On A-Hurtin.'”

Johnny is originated from United States. He joined Ray Charles as a member of the Florida Artists Hall of Fame.
