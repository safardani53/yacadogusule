Katy Perry Reveals Kristen Stewart Text Message, Laments Media Focus on Her Nipples

For the record: Katy Perry never hooked up with Robert Pattinson.

Yes, there were rumors of these two hanging out and watching random wedding together.

There was even talk that Perry encouraged Pattinson to dump Kristen Stewart.

But Perry tells the latest issue of Elle UK that nothing ever happened between her and the Twilight Saga star and that she felt bad when rumors to the contrary spread online.

She even sent Stewart a text message when the talk was at its apex.

 

What did it say?

I know you’ve seen all this stuff but you know I would never disrespect you. I’m not that person. I’m just trying to be a friend to him but it is unfortunate that I do have a set of tits.

Unfortunate that the media focuses on that body part and presumes there’s a romance attached to her relationship with Pattinson, the singer meant.

The friendship between Katy and Rob goes back awhile. The latter even helped the former through her divorce from Russell Brand.

As for her current beau, John Mayer? Perry admits the two called it quits in March and then made up in June and explains why:

“He pulled away. That was a big hello for me. I realized I could lose the person I loved and I had to deal with some things, issues I think a lot of women have. You can be strong in one aspect of your life but submissive in another.”
