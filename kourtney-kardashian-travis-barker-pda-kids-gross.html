KARDASHIAN fans have slammed Kourtney and her husband Travis Barker for their frequent PDA around their kids.

The newlyweds, who share six children from their previous relationships, are often seen kissing and hugging in public.

Speaking on Amanda Hirsch’s Not Skinny But Not Fat podcast on Tuesday, 43-year-old Kourtney opened up about her and 46-year-old Travis’ home life.

The pair share a total of six children, including Kourtney’s three young kids from her relationship with Scott Disick, 39, and Travis’ three older kids from his marriage to Shanna Moakler, 47.

“So you’re all over each other, all day, for real?” asked Amanda.

“Yes, but we try to be respectful too of the kids and want everyone to feel comfortable,” the Lemme founder replied.
