Kristen Wiig Appears on The Tonight Show as Khaleesi, Doesnt Really Know Anything About Khaleesi

We don’t mean to sound hyperbolic or anything, but what follows is the funniest video in the history of mankind.

Kristen Wiig appeared on The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon last night, dressing and acting like Daenerys Targaryen from Game of Thrones.

Except we’re not really sure if Wiig has seen much of Game of Thrones Season 5, or any season for that matter, as she seemed a tad unsure about various Khaleesi-related facts.

No, Dany doesn’t live in a dome in a forest on a mountain, Kristen. Nor do we we think her favorite food is "meat."

But we do agree with this version of Dany: Kristen Wiig is pretty awesome and her new film does sound like it has a "great premise."

The Saturday Night live alumnus has a habit of dressing as other people when stopped by Fallon’s talk show.

Last year, she masqueraded as Harry Styles of One Direction, while in 2013, she dressed as Michael Jordan when Fallon was host of "Late Night."

We never thought anything would top Mark-Paul Gosselaar coming out on to The Tonight Show stage as Zack Morris, but here we have it.

This take on The Mother of Dragons is mother effin awesome.
