Lim Ju-hwan Biography

SBS Drama Awards winner actor, Lim Ju-hwan is well known for his leading role in the television drama "Tamra, the Island".

Lim Ju-hwan's early life

He was born on May 18, 1982, in Seoul, South Korea. Lim Ju-hwan completed his graduation from high school and later he enrolled at Daejin University for his undergraduate and graduate studies in theatre and film.
