Love Island star Molly Marsh reveals huge career change five months after leaving show

MOLLY Marsh has revealed a huge career change just five months after Love Island.

The 22-year-old made it to the Love Island final with Zachariah Noble in July.

And in a new YouTube video the social media influencer told fans she's trying out something new.

Molly said: "Tomorrow’s gonna be a super fun day, I’m actually gonna bring you on a voiceover job with me in the morning."

The next day Molly returned to the camera and added: "Just got back to the hotel, my session went so well! I did the whole thing in under an hour which was kind of rubbish because you get paid hourly.

"It’s gonna be on the radio in January. If you hear me on the radio talking about life skills, it’s me."

More on Love Island

Molly and Zach, 25, are set to spend their first Christmas together and he recently revealed he's going away with her whole family.

He told OK!: "On Boxing Day, I'm away with Molly and her family for a few days. They were talking about some type of log cabin.

"I don't know the location, I don't really know the ins and outs but I think it's a tradition for them or they've gone before, so proper Christmas.

"I didn't think of that, that it's our first Christmas, it feels like I've been with her for 10 years."
