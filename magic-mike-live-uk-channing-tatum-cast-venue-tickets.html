Magic Mike Live tickets on sale NOW! Cast, dates and venue for the Channing Tatum show

MAGIC Mike is coming to tour the UK!

But when is it on? And is Channing Tatum involved? Here's all you need to know...

When does Magic Mike Live open in the UK? What are the dates?

The show kicks of from November 10, 2018, you can catch it:

  • Every Tuesday to Sunday at 7.30pm
  • There is an extra performance every Thursday to Sunday at 10pm

Please note that Magic Mike Live is strictly an OVER 18's ONLY event.

Due to the audiences overwhelming demand for tickets, the show has now been extended with a second booking period from April to October 2019.

How do I get a ticket to Magic Mike Live UK?

Tickets are on sale now.

They start at £29 an go up to £105 - plus booking fee.

You can buy them at Ticketmaster.

Is Channing Tatum involved in Magic Mike UK?

After taking Las Vegas by storm, Channing Tatum is bringing the brand-new production of Magic Mike Live to the stage in London.

The Hollywood hunk - who penned the two films Magic Mike and Magic Mike XXL - is the director of the show.

Channing launched the show on the Britain's Got Talent 2018 final.
