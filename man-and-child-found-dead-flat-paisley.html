Man and child found dead at flat in Paisley, Scotland, as police probe 'unexplained' deaths

A MAN and child have been found dead at a flat.

The pair were tragically discovered at the home in Paisley, Scotland, around 8pm on Monday night.

Cops were called to the scene on the town's Argyle Street after concerns were raised.

The body of a man and boy were then found inside the flat.

Forensics have also been spotted at the scene during the week.

Their deaths are currently being treated as "unexplained" and post mortems are due to take place.

Police say there is nothing to suggest anyone else was involved in the death.

A Police Scotland spokesperson said: “We received a report of concern for a man and a child at a property in Argyle Street, Paisley around 8pm on Monday, 15 May, 2023.

“Officers attended and a man and a boy were found dead within the property.

"The deaths are currently being treated as unexplained and post mortem examinations will take place in due course.
