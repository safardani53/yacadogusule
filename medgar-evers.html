Who is Medgar Evers:

Medgar Evers is a famous Civil Rights Leader. He was born on July 2, 1925 and his birthplace is Decatur, MS. Medgar is also well known as, Civil rights activist during the Civil Rights Movement who advocated against segregation and was the first field secretary for the Mississippi chapter of the NAACP. The fact that the trial of his murderer, Byron De La Beckwith, resulted in two hung juries caused much anger in the African American community.

Medgar is originated from United States. He was assassinated hours after John F. Kennedy’s speech supporting civil rights.
