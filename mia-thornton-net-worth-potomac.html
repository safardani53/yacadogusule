Who is Mia Thornton from Real Housewives of Potomac?

Mia Thornton is an entrepreneur known for her role on The Real Housewives of Potomac.

The housewife has accumulated not only a beautiful family but a considerable net worth.

Who is Mia Thornton?

Mia Thornton is a cast member on Bravo's Real Housewives of Potomac.

She was born on November 25, 1984, and is known as an entrepreneur, franchise owner, and regional developer. 

After graduating from Southeastern Institute, the reality TV star received a degree in Neuromuscular Massage Therapy.

She serves on the board and supports families of the Ronald McDonald House of Charlotte, A Better World, UNCF, Autism Speaks, and A Child’s Place.

What is Mia Thornton's net worth?

The entrepreneur is reported to have a net worth of around $5million, per ExactNetWorth.

Her extravagant lifestyle is afforded by her career, as she began working at a clinic in North Carolina after graduating from college.

She then eventually became an owner of some Massage Envy locations.

She also founded Amilleon London, a cosmetics company.

Is Mia Thornton married?

Mia is married to Gordon Thornton from North Carolina.

Gordon was born on March 18, 1954.

Mia has been married to her Gordon since 2012.

He is reported to have a net worth of approximately $100million.

The star's husband began accumulating wealth as a franchise owner of multiple Mcdonald’s franchises in 1993.

The couple shares their Maryland penthouse with their three children, Joshua, Jeremiah, and Juliana.
