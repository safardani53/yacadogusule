Michelle Rodriguez Hollywood Life

Bio:
Michelle Rodriguez (born Mayte Michelle Rodríguez in San Antonio, Texas on July 12, 1978) is an American film and TV actress. She made her debut in the independent film Girlfight, and she was recognized by the National Board of Review. She went on to do many very popular roles like The Fast and The Furious, Blue Crush and S.W.A.T. For 2005 and 2006, she starred on the hit TV series, Lost. Other notable movies for Michelle include Machete, Battle: Los Angeles and Resident Evil: Retribution.

Best Known For:
Michelle Rodriguez is best known for playing Letty Ortiz in The Fast and The Furious movies.

Personal Life:
Michelle Rodriguez opened up in 2006 to Cosmopolitan magazine about having “experimented with both sexes.” Then in 2013, in an interview with Entertainment Weekly, she said that yes, she had “gone both ways.” Romantically, she has been paired with Vin Diesel and Olivier Martinez. In 2006, it was rumored after comments by her co-star, that she was involved with Kristanna Loken.
