Who is Mike Alstott:

Mike Alstott is a famous Football Player. He was born on December 21, 1973 and his birthplace is Joliet, IL. Mike is also well known as, Former NFL fullback who played his entire 11-year career with the Tampa Bay Buccaneers.

Mike is originated from United States. He was teammates with Ronde Barber during the Buccaneers Super Bowl XXXVII victory.
