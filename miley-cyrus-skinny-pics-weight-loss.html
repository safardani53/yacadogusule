[PICS] Miley Cyrus Weight Loss Down 30 Pounds, Shes Stick Thin Hollywood Life

The 19-year-old stepped out in Miami looking scary skinny in a grey dress that hung off her tiny frame. Her legs looked stick thin, her collarbones popped and her waist looked noticeably smaller.
