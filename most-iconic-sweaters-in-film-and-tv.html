The 22 Most Iconic Sweaters in Film and TV

Marilyn Monroe's Sweater in Let's Make Love

The bombshell's light purple cable knit and black tights look while singing the song "My Heart Belongs to Daddy" continues to be one of her most memorable ever.

Cary Grant's Striped Sweater in To Catch a Thief

The legendary actor's classic stripes paired perfectly with a silk scarf in the 1955 film.

Faye Dunaway's Short-Sleeve Sweater in Bonnie and Clyde

The actress's wardrobe in this 1967 film, in which she played one half of the infamous killer couple, remains one of the all-time best.

Mr. Rogers' Red Cardigan

Just looking at Fred Rogers' signature cardigan makes us smile.

Velma's Sweater on Scooby Doo

Jinkies! That chunky orange turtleneck sweater is synonymous with solving mysteries, but there's no mystery when it comes to this look — Velma knew what color worked for her and stuck to it.

Olivia Newton-John's Sunny Cardigan in Grease

Tell me more, tell me more about how much you love Sandy's yellow cardigan — which matched her skirt and her blonde hair! — in the 1978 musical.

Meg Ryan & Billy Crystal in When Harry Met Sally

There's so much to love about the complementary sweaters that Meg Ryan and Billy Crystal wear in the beloved rom-com. Who could blame Sally for falling for Harry when he rocks such a good chunky knit?

This Striped Sweater That Haunts Our Dreams

Horizontal stripes terrify us to this day thanks to Freddy Krueger's striped sweater from Nightmare on Elm Street.

The Off-the-Shoulder Sweatshirt in Flashdance

What a feeling! The asymmetrical gray sweatshirt that Jennifer Beals wore in Flashdance perfectly represents all things '80s.

Drew Barrymore's Sweater in Scream

Barrymore made her big comeback in 1996's Scream — and famously lasted all of 12 minutes on screen. No matter, this sweater still made an impact! So much so that Barrymore wore it again for a spoof on the film in October 2020.

This Ugly Christmas Sweater in Bridget Jones's Diary

We love anything Colin Firth wears, but his character Mark Darcy's reindeer sweater lives in our heads rent-free.

Harry & Ron's Christmas Sweaters

These sweaters from Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone made us wish that we went to Hogwarts and got homemade crewnecks from Mrs. Weasley for Christmas.

Ross's (and Tag's) Red Sweater in Friends

Sure, it made the wearer look like they had randomly placed sweat stains — but it was also the clue that got everybody talking about who Rachel's baby daddy was.

Liv Tyler's Cropped Sweater in Empire Records

The entirety of Corey Mason's (played by Liv Tyler) outfit here — from the plaid miniskirt to the cropped sweater — is making a comeback. And for good reason!

Jeff Bridge's Sweater in The Big Lebowski

The Dude's patterned sweater from the 1998 comedy is so iconic, even Aaron Rodgers wore it.

Damian's Hoodie in Mean Girls

On Wednesdays, we're supposed to wear pink, but we'd rather wear this disguise that Damian (Daniel Franzese) wore to shout, "She doesn't even go here!" in 2004's Mean Girls.

This Multi-Colored Sweater in Love Actually

Love Actually has good and bad romances and good and bad "jumpers," but this one worn by Andrew Lincoln is probably the worst offender.

Killmonger's Killer Cardigan in Black Panther

We know he's supposed to be a villain in this movie, but we're convinced there's not a sweater in the world that Michael B. Jordan couldn't make look great.
