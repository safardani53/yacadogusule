The Best Kisses Ever Hollywood Life

The MTV Movie Awards include fabulous red carpet pre shows, awards featuring the year’s best films and all of the celebs dressed to the nines! The awards have been hosted by A-list celebs like Lindsay Lohan, Zachary Levi & more. As well, the show is always packed with prime entertainment, and has had performances by Marin Garrix, The Beastie Boys & many other artists. 

There’s always a ton of PDA packed into award shows, both on and off stage! At the 2014 awards, Paris Hilton (left) gave Carmen Electra a big smooch in front of the audience after presenting Electra with the award for ‘Best Kiss’ for her role in ‘Starsky & Hutch’.
