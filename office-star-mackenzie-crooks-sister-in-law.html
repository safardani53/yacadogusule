COPS have found a body while searching for the sister-in-law of The Office star Mackenzie Crook.

Laurel Aldridge, 62, was last seen in the Walberton area, near Arundel, in West Sussex on Tuesday February 14.

Police had launched a frantic search for Laurel, who has been undergoing chemotherapy.

Today they confirmed they found the body of a woman in the Tortington Lane area of Arundel.

A spokesperson said: "At this stage, the family of missing Laurel Aldridge have been informed and we are seeking to confirm the identity of the woman."

Mackenzie, 51, this week begged for the public's help in finding his sister-in-law.
