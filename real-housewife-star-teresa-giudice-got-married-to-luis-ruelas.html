Real Housewife Star Teresa Giudice Got Married To Luis Ruelas

Teresa Giudice married Luis Ruelas on August 6 at the Park Chateau Estate & Gardens in East Brunswick, New Jersey, with 220 guests in attendance. Giudice claimed in her wedding vows, “I will love you for a million tomorrows,” according to The Daily Mail. They also lighted a candle before their ceremonial wedding kiss, with Rihanna’s “Shine Bright Like a Diamond” playing in the background. The couple was then legally married. Teresa’s four children and Luis’ two boys stood by their sides as the couple exchanged vows, with Ruelas’ sister Dr. Veronica Ruelas officiating the wedding.

Everything we know about the children of Teresa Giudice and Luis Ruelas

Teresa and Luis got engaged in October 2021, after the latter dreamily proposed to Giudice while down on one knee. Teresa’s four kids with ex-husband Joe Giudice; Gia, Gabriella, Milania, and Audriana, and Luis’ two sons with ex-wife Marisa DiMartino; Louie Jr. and Nicholas, are set to begin their blended family after a beautiful wedding. The two sons attended the wedding to be with their father and to show their support for the newlyweds.

Luis’ son Louie Jr. is supposedly no longer living with the mixed family since he began college last year. However, his autistic son, Nicholas, lives at home as part of a mixed family. Luis is also known to have helped raise donations for autistic youngsters. Before Teresa and Luis sealed the wedding, the mixed family enjoyed its share of fun and laughter. Teresa’s girls celebrated the 18th birthday of Luis’ son Louis Jr. when their parents moved in together. Teresa took to Instagram to share a tale with her mixed family, captioning it, “Louie, happy 18th birthday!!! It was a pleasure to celebrate your birthday with you.”

Teresa Giudice’s oldest daughter Gia had uploaded another photo from the festivities with the comment, “Louie, happy birthday. Congratulations on your new sisters.” Teresa Giudice often shares adorable photos of her kids on social media. On Instagram, she acknowledged her love for her girls and posted an exquisite snapshot from her wedding day. The caption reads, “Built-in best friends…To the moon and back, I adore you gals!” Teresa expressed gratitude for the four daughters she has with Joe Giudice.

More about Teresa and Luis’ new family home

Teresa Giudice and Luis Ruelas bought their mixed family a new house years before the wedding, and it is just lovely! Gia Giudice disclosed information about her gorgeous nine-bedroom mansion on a 6-acre tract to Reality Tidbit. Gia and Teresa Giudice are enthused over their property, which allegedly costs $3.4 million and has a separate bedroom for each of Teresa and Luis’ children, as well as an additional room. Teresa Giudice expressed her eagerness to show her children the home in an interview with The Real Housewives of New Jersey. She said in her opening statement:

“We have done several modifications since purchasing the home… So I’m really looking forward to the girls seeing their new home.”

Despite the sad aspect of moving into a new house, Giudice’s girls and Luis’ son Louie were blown away by the property, according to Bravo TV.
