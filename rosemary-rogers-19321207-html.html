Rosemary Rogers, née Jansz is a bestselling British historical romance novel. Her first book, Sweet Savage Love, was published in 1974. She was the second romantic author, after Kathleen Woodiwiss, to have her novel published in paperback format. Both writers had initial success working with publisher Nancy Coffey, who was then working for Avon Books. Rogers is considered one of the founders of the modern historical romance, and many writers today consider his work one of the most influential of them. She lives in California.
