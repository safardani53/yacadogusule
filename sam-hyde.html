Sam Hyde Biography

Sam Hyde, born Samuel Whitcomb Hyde, is a prominent American comedian, actor, and writer. He is most popular for his comedy group named Million Dollar Extreme.

Sam Hyde was born in 1985, in Fall River, Massachusetts, U.S. After graduating from B.M.C. Durfee High School, he enrolled at Carnegie Mellon University.

Hyde spent one year at Carnegie Mellon before transferring to the Rhode Island School of Design, where he graduated in 2007 with a B.A. in Film, Animation, and Video.
