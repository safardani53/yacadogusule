Samsung Galaxy S23 and S23+ deals - where to buy the new phone

ANDROID fans, there's a brand-new phone on the market: Samsung's latest handset has just gone on general sale.

Read on to find out where you can buy the Samsung Galaxy S23, S23+ and S23 Ultra.

The latest flagship Galaxy S23 was revealed at the Samsung Unpacked event earlier this month.

The brand-new line of phones includes the standard Samsung Galaxy S23, along with its bigger brother the S23+ and baddest of the bunch, the S23 Ultra.

And it comes with all the cutting edge specs and features you would expect from the world-renowned tech brand.

The all-new Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 internal processor offers some serious horsepower across all the new devices.
