James Shannon Larkin is an American musician, best known as the drummer of rock band Godsmack. He replaced the previous drummer Tommy Stewart in June 2002. Prior to Godsmack, he was a member of Amen, Candlebox, Ugly Kid Joe, and Souls at Zero. Shannon played drums for Godsmack for their albums Faceless, IV, The Other Side EP, The Oracle, 1000hp, and their latest album When Legends Rise.
