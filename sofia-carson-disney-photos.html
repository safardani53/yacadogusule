Photos Of The Disney Channel Star Hollywood Life

Sofia Carson is constantly serving looks, both on and off the red carpet! The actress, who is best known for her starring role in the Descendants trilogy, and the series Pretty Little Liars: The Perfectionists, frequently turns heads at events and awards shows.

Sofia most recently stole the spotlight at the 2019 Latin Grammy Awards when she wore a hot pink dress with a short skirt in the front that tapered to a long back including a dramatic caped train. It featured ruffles all along the hem, as well as on the cuff of her long sleeves and high neck. She wore her dark hair pulled back as to let the gown get full attention. 

Pink is certainly one of her favorite colors to rock on the red carpet! She opted for a light pink gown by Giambattista Valli that was absolutely fit for a princess, at the 77th Annual Golden Globe Awards in January 2020.
