Stefanos Tsitsipas (Tennis Player) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Stefanos Tsitsipas is a Greek professional tennis player. He is the youngest tennis professional ranked in the top 10 by the Professional Tennis Association and has a high ranking in his career as world number 5, making him the highest ranked Greek player in history. Tsitsipas was the 2019 ATP Finals champion, becoming the youngest player to win the year-end championship in eighteen years. He won six ATP singles titles. Born into a tennis family where his mother Julia Apostoli was a tour expert for the Women's Tennis Association and his father trained as a tennis coach, Tsitsipas knew the sport at the age of three and started taking lessons from the age of six. When he was a junior he was ranked number 1 in the world. He also became the third Greek player, and the first Greek male player of the open era, to win a smaller Grand Slam title with a men's doubles victory at Wimbledon 2016. Tsitsipas has been trained at the Patrick Mouratoglou Academy in France since 2015
