The Girl With the Tramp Stamp Tattoo Trailer: Terrifyingly Trashy

By now, you’ve likely seen The Girl With the Dragon Tattoo trailer. Now prepare yourself for The Girl With the Tramp Stamp Tattoo … a truly nightmarish spoof.

There is a pink scooter. There is projectile vomit spurred by Jagermeister. Most importantly, there is a huge butterfly tattoo on the lower back of Emma Roberts.

Watching her W.T. version of “Lisbeth” is so classic that it may ruin your anticipation for the real thing, or make it harder to take seriously – the goal of any parody.
