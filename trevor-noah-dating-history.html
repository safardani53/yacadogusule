A Look at His Relationships

'The Daily Show' host Trevor Noah may be upfront about his career, but what about his dating history? Details on Trevor's past girlfriends here.

Comedian Trevor Noah has been in the public eye for years now, and with that experience in the spotlight comes plenty of questions about his personal life. Namely, people want to know about who he has dated in the past ... and who he's dating now.
