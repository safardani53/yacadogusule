Katerina Graham Is 'Not Complaining' About Scenes With Steven R. McQueen! Hollywood Life

And in case you had ANY concerns that the cast wasn’t buddy-buddy off-camera, you can officially put those fears to bed. Katerina told us the entire cast is “very close.”

“[If we shot in LA] I think we would all be so busy and have so many responsibilities press-wise,” she said. “But because we’re in Georgia, we’re just like, ‘Hey guys, what do you want to do after work? I don’t know. Let’s go hang out. Let’s go to a bar or a movie.’ It’s a very normal environment.”

She told us specifically that she loves “hanging out at Candice’s house and talking to Nina We’re all just really close. These are my girls.”

As if we needed yet another reason to love these girls. Anybody have a spare room in Mystic Falls? I’m looking to rent!

— Andy Swift, reporting by Michelle Skowronek
