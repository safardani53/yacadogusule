Welsh cottage for sale in Trefor, Gwynedd, at 475,000 and comes with its own beach

A STUNNING four-bed Welsh cottage has gone on sale for £475,000 - and comes complete with its own beach.

Morfa Cottage in Trefor, Gwynedd, is nestled in the beautiful Welsh countryside and has 11 acres of woodland and fields.

But for just the asking price of £475,000, owners could have their own section of shingle beach on the North Wales coastal path.

The 17th Century Grade II listed property also boasts four bedrooms and a traditional country kitchen with an AGA.

There are also two study rooms and the home retains many of its original features with a modern touch.

It also offers some of the best views of the surrounding landscape.

Hugh O'Donnell, Rural Associate at Carter Jonas, said: "With its magnificent views of the Welsh countryside, it's no surprise that Morfa has been attracting interest from Aberdeen to London, and everywhere in between.

"It's quintessential charm, access to the coast and the Llyn Peninsula, and very reasonable price tag make Morfa a tranquil haven, whether buyers are looking for a place to spend the holidays or a complete lifestyle change."
