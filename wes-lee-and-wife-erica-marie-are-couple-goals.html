Deveon Everhart Aikens, born November 2, 1994, is an American professional wrestler.

Wes is also popular for being a member of The Rascalz, which was formed in 2018, aligning with Trey Miguel and Zachary Wentz.

Wes Lee Wife Erica Marie

Wes Lee and his wife Erica Marie shared their wedding vows in December 2020. The wife of the professional WWE star is a fitness and dance instructor.

Born February 11, 1991, Erica Marie started her professional journey in the entertainment world as an NBA dancer and cheerleader.

Later, she tried her hands on diverse career avenues, including modeling, acting, and singing.

Eventually, she found her way to channel her passion for dance as a fitness instructor at “CTY COMMIT Dance Fitness.”

Additionally, she is closely associated with “Bella Entertainment,” an organization known for providing cheerleaders and dancers for parties and entertainment events.

Moreover, she has a considerable social media presence with more than 4k followers on her Instagram.
