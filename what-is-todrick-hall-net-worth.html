What is Todrick Hall's net worth?

TODRICK Hall is a singer, songwriter, and choreographer known for his online persona.

The 36-year-old has over 3.62million YouTube subscribers.

What is Todrick Hall's net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Todrick Hall has an estimated net worth of around $4million.

After making it to the semi-finals on season nine of American Idol, Todrick established himself as a popular YouTuber and LGBTQ+ advocate.

He was named a resident choreographer and occasional judge on RuPaul's Drag Race during season eight of the series.

The star performed on Broadway between 2016 and 2017, starring in Kinky Boots and Chicago.

How many albums has Todrick Hall released?

Todrick Hall has released four studio albums since his debut into the music scene, including:

  • Somebody's Christmas (2010)
  • Straight Outta Oz (2016)
  • Forbidden (2018)
  • Femuline (2021)

Both Straight Outta Oz and Forbidden double as visual albums.

He also dropped a Covid-19 related EP in 2020, entitled Quarantine Queen.

Who is Todrick Hall?

Born on April 4, 1985, Todrick Hall is a native of Texas.

While in elementary school, Todrick found himself involved in the theatre, orchestra, and ballet.

From a young age, he was a fan of The Wizard of Oz - later in life, he went on to create Oz, The Musical; The Wizard of Ahhhs; and Straight Out of Oz.
