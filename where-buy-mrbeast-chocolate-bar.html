Where to buy MrBeast's chocolate bar

JIMMY "MrBeast" Donaldson has unveiled yet another new project.

The YouTuber has launched a line of chocolate bars called Feastables that put buyers in with a chance of winning some crazy prizes.

Where to buy MrBeast's chocolate bar

With over 89 million subscribers on his main channel alone, MrBeast is one of the most popular YouTubers on the site right now.

He’s set up several other ventures outside YouTube including his popular MrBeast burger chain, but on January 29, 2022, he announced yet another new project.

Feastables chocolate bars are available to purchase online, but according to the website the company currently only ships within the US.

To purchase a bar, head to the Feastables website.

You can buy Original Chocolate, Quinoa Crunch, Almond Chocolate, and you can also get a variety pack.

The website states that the bars are made with organic ingredients and no artificial flavors.

The four key ingredients are sugar cane, cocoa bean, cocoa butter and sunflower lecithin.

It is not known yet when other countries will be selling Feastables yet.

How can I win a mystery ticket in MrBeast's chocolate bars?

To check to see whether you have won any prizes, peel the sticker on the back of the packet when it arrives to retrieve your code.

Scan the QR code using your phone, and then enter your contact details and code in the online form.

Then, spin the wheel to see if you’ve been lucky enough to win something.

What do you win with the mystery ticket?

Jimmy is giving away over $1million worth of prizes, and ten random people who win a "mystery ticket" in their bar will be flown out to compete in a video for his “chocolate factory”,

Other prizes including Teslas, Sea-Doos, Super73 electric bikes, Artesian Builds gaming PCs, Turtle Beach gaming bundles, Beats by Dre earphones, a lifetime of chocolate, cash plus MrBeast merch.

In the coming months, Jimmy will ask ten lucky Grand Prize winners to compete in a MrBeast video for a chance to win a copy of MrBeast’s Chocolate Factory.
