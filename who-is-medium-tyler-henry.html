Who is medium Tyler Henry?

TYLER Henry made headlines in 2022 following the release of his new Netflix show, Life After Death with Tyler Henry.

Now, fans want to know more about the clairvoyant medium and where else they have seen the star before.

Who is medium Tyler Henry?

Born January 13, 1996, Henry, 26, is known as an American reality show personality and clairvoyant medium.

Henry's career as a medium involves a number of things including predicting the future as well as communicating between spirits of the dead and those who are living.

He had reportedly first learned he had psychic abilities when he was just 10-years-old after receiving intuitive mental images foretelling his grandmother's death.

Henry is also a published author of the books Between Two Worlds: Lessons from the Other Side, and Here & Hereafter: How Wisdom from the Departed Can Transform Your Life Now, which is scheduled to be released on March 29, 2022.

Prior to his Netflix show, Henry was the host of the TV series Hollywood Medium which was first released in 2016.

What is Tyler's show about?

Life After Death with Tyler Henry is Netflix's newest reality show which follows Henry across the country as he offers readings to those on his waiting list.

more from netflix

"Clairvoyant medium Tyler Henry offers clarity and closure from the beyond while searching through his own family’s past in an intimate reality series," the show's descriptions reads.

Season one has nine episodes which are available to stream now exclusively on Netflix.

"I am so excited and grateful for this show. It has given me the opportunity to bring answers and healing to so many people around the country. It allowed me to truly change people’s lives," he wrote on his website about the show.

"I also had no idea I would be exploring a family mystery of my own. I am so proud of the profound stories, true testimonials of resilience, and shocking validations that made this such a rewarding experience for me.”

Prior to the show's release, Henry sat down with Today host Jenna Bush on March 9, 2022, to give a reading after he had successfully predicted the gender of her baby back in 2016.

Henry talked about Bush's late grandfather, George H. W. Bush as well as her late grandmother, Jenna Hawkins Welch, and her husband's late father, John Hager.

At the end of the reading, Bush asked if he saw any more children in her future to which he responded, "I hope if you want some that you do, but I'm just not seeing [any]."

"That's good," Bush said. "I think three feels good."
