Who is Reggie Bush's wife Lilit Avagyan?

PROFESSIONAL dancer and choreographer Lilit Avagyan married football commentator Reggie Bush in 2014.

A former NFL running back, Reggie Bush played for the Miami Dolphins, Detroit Lions, Buffalo Bills, and San Francisco 49ers.

Who is Lilit Avagryan?

Lilt Avagyan is an Armenian dancer, choreographer, and dance teacher. In 2010, Avagyan won the Rising Star Dance Competition after she studied dance for more than 12 years in Europe.

Three years later, the 33-year-old dancer went on to compete in the Latin American World Championships in Australia.

Avagyan did go on to guest star on Dancing with The Stars, although videos of her short time on the show aren’t widely available.

How did Lilit Avagyan and Reggie Bush meet?

Bush had a series of girlfriends before meeting Avagyan in 2011. In 2007 he began dating Kim Kardashian West, but their relationship fizzled out after two years.

He then dated country singer Jesse James in 2010 before he met Avagyan a year later. The couple dated for two years before making their way down the aisle after the birth of their first child.

The wedding was held in San Diego, California at the Grand Del Mar Hotel and welcomed celebrity guests including Brody Jenner, the step-brother of Bush’s ex-girlfriend Kardashian West.

Do they have any kids?

Bush and Avagyan now have three children, having welcomed their first daughter Briseis Avagyan Bush into the world on May 6, 2013.

Their son Uriah is now six years old and their youngest daughter, Agyemang, is now four years old.

In 2020, Bush retired from the NFL to focus on their family life. "I've been able to enjoy more time with our family and just being able to do things that I normally wouldn't be able to be here for,” he told People in 2020.

“For 20-plus years, most of my time was devoted to a single sport. Now I get an opportunity to be home, and to find a new passion and keep my focus on top of my family."

He spoke about the struggles and growing pains of homeschooling their children and said, “It's definitely been a challenge … but together we feel like we've been learning in this process a lot about ourselves, and we're making it work.”
