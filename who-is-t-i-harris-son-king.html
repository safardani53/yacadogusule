Who is T.I. Harris' son King?

KING Harris is the son of famous American rapper TI, but little is known about him.

He appeared in the news after an argument with his dad, but who is he, and who are his parents?

Who is King Harris?

King Harris was born in 2004 and is the son of rap legend T.I.

He is active on social media and has an army of more than 800,000 followers.

He regularly posts photos and videos of himself rapping and performing on stage.

King made headlines after he "disrespected" his famous Dad, T.I, on Instagram.

The rant followed an argument which had broken out between the pair as they watched the Atlanta Falcons’ divisional matchup against the New Orleans Saints.

Who is King Harris' dad T.I.?

T.I was born in 1980 in Atlanta, Georgia.

His full name is Clifford Joseph Harris Jr.

He is known as one of the pioneers of the hip hop subgenre trap music.

He signed his first major record deal in 1999 and has had many hit tracks since, including What You Know and Still Ain’t Forgave Myself.

T.I has also tried his hand at acting and has worked as an executive producer for music projects other than his own.

These include New Orleans-based rapper B.G.'s tenth album Too Hood 2 Be Hollywood (2009) and Atlanta-based rapper B.o.B's debut album The Adventures of Bobby Ray.

Who is King Harris' mom Tiny?

Tiny Harris - also know as - Tameka Cottle was born in 1975 in College Park, Georgia.

Tiny rose to fame in the  1990s as a member of the multi-platinum R&B vocal group Xscape and recieved a Grammy for her writing contributions to the  TLC hit "No Scrubs."

She began dating T.I in 2001 and they married in 2010 in a lavish ceremony on Miami beach.

Tiny shares three children with T.I., King Harris , Major Philant/Phillip Harris and Heiress Diana Harris - King is the oldest.

Both Tiny and T.I have kids from previous relationships.

Tiny’s firstborn daughter, Zonnique Jailee Pullins was born in 1996, followed by T.I's firstborn son Messiah Ya’Majesty Harris, who was born in 2000.
