Who is Tamar Braxton? The US Sun

TAMAR Braxton has been in the spotlight since the 1990s, when the popular R&B singing group, The Braxtons, was formed.

Since then, Tamar has come a long way and continues to grow both her music and television resumes.

Who is Tamar Braxton?

Tamar Braxton is a popular singer and television personality who was born on March 17, 1977, in Severn, Maryland.

She first rose to fame in 1990 after she formed The Braxtons alongside her sisters, Traci Braxton, Trina Braxton, and Toni Braxton.

The trio released their debut album, So Many Ways, in 1996 but disbanded shortly after.

Tamar then went on to start a successful solo career but reunited with her family in 2011 for the popular reality series Braxton Family Values, which ran until 2020.

Over the years, Tamar has won several awards, including one BET Award and three Soul Train Music Awards.

She also made history on Celebrity Big Brother by becoming the first African American to win in the United States.

Tamar will now star in VH1's reboot of the popular reality series The Surreal Life in October 2022.

Is Tamar Braxton married?

Over the years, Tamar has walked down the aisle twice.

She first married composer Darrell "Delite" Allamby in 2001, but they divorced in 2003.

Five years later, she then tied the knot with Streamline Records founder Vincent Herbert.

The two were married until 2019, during which time they welcomed a son, Logan Vincent Herbert.

Since her second divorce, Tamar has kept her dating life out of the spotlight, but she has since been linked to David Adefeso and Jeremy Robinson.

What is Tamar Braxton's net worth?

Due to Tamar's successful career in the entertainment industry, she has been able to amass a fortune over the years.

As of October 2022, her net worth is an estimated $3million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

This estimate comes from the success of her music career as well as her television success.
