Wow! Jessica Alba Hits First Red Carpet After Baby

One month after giving birth to daughter Haven Garner, Jessica Alba is already back on the red carpet and she's looking more gorgeous than ever.

The actress, 30, attended the 2011 Alma Awards in Santa Monica, California on Sunday night in gray print strapless Michael Kors gown, which was  cinched at the waist with a silver belt. She accessorized with a silver clutch and a gold studded necklace.

Recently, Alba announced that she was kicking off her post-baby slimdown.

"I did my first day of workout since I had the baby," Alba tweeted Aug. 30. "Just cardio for 40 minutes."

The svelte star explained that she got the A-OK from her physician. "My doc told me I could start after 2 wks," she wrote. "I'm going light, stationary bike and elliptical."
