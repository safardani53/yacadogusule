Who is Xavier McDaniel:

Xavier McDaniel is a famous Basketball Player. He was born on June 4, 1963 and his birthplace is Columbia, SC. Xavier is also well known as, Forward who was nicknamed X-Man, played professionally in the NBA and in Greece from 1985 to 1998. He was named to his first and only NBA All-Star game as a member of the Seattle SuperSonics in 1988.

Xavier is originated from United States. He played alongside Patrick Ewing on the New York Knicks during the 1991-92 season.
