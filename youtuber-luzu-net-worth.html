Who is YouTuber Luzu and what's his net worth?

LUZU has become a well known star in the world of social media.

But who is he and how did he rise to fame? Here's everything you need to know about the Spanish social media sensation.

Who is YouTuber Luzu?

Luzu, born August 3, 1985, is a Spanish-born YouTuber.

He is one of the video platforms most viewed Spanish speaking content creators.

His YouTube channel, @luzugames, was first created in May 2013, and as the name suggests, the popular page focuses on gaming content.

As of June 2023, the content creator boasts a staggering 11.2 million subscribers.

But his YouTube journey doesn't stop there.

Away from gaming, Luzu has created a number of other channels, one of which is @LuzuVlogs, which has an impressive 2.55 million subscribers.

As a result of his success, Luzu has collaborated with several other online creators, including Caspar Lee, Rubius, and WillyRex.

He has also appeared in a number of popular ''react'' videos - where internet stars film their reactions to something that they’ve never seen before.

As well as gaining notoriety on YouTube, Luzu has also amassed millions of followers on Twitter, Twitch and Instagram.

His audience on his channels, and his streams, are mainly Spanish-speaking, due to this being Luzu's native language.

As well as finding fame and success, Luzu has also found love.

The social media star married his wife, Lana, in 2019 and the pair share a son.

How did Luzu become famous?

Like many influencers and content creators, Luzu found success on social media.

Thanks to his YouTube channels, he has amassed millions of fans and followers who regularly interact with his content.

Prior to becoming a social media sensation, Luzu studied communication, followed by a job at a Spanish TV production company.

What is Luzu's net worth?

Over the years, YouTube has been a trailblazer among social media platforms to help its creators monetize their content.

As a result, some of the biggest YouTubers have become multi-millionaires.
